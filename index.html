My Research

My research focuses on computational imaging system with meta-optics, combining metalens imaging system design, wave propagation modeling, computational image reconstruction with deep learning–based algorithms as well as experiment testing. I enjoy tackling complex problems in optical and computational imaging, combining physics, algorithm design, and experimental techniques, and contributing to collaborative research environments.

I began my research during my undergraduate studies at Sun Yat-sen University, where I worked on integrated photonics device design. This experience helped me realize how much I enjoy research, which led me to pursue a Ph.D. Outside of research, I am a classical piano enthusiast, which is a fulfilling way to spend my spare time.
